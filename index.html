Vaelora — Chronicles of the Four Worlds

About the Project

Chronicles of the Four Worlds is a fantasy-sci-fi multiverse project. Four worlds are connected via the Avatar system, shared lore, and a global threat — the Nilids, gods who devour universes. The project develops across books, anime, games, and media.

Worlds & Gods

Main World: Reality shifts with power zones; technology fails, humans evolve as mages and cultivators. (God: Vaelora — Love & Unity)

Magical World: Towers of knowledge, magical duels, beast summoning, village defense. (God: Sophion — Knowledge & Reason)

Techno World: Cyborgs, genetic enhancements, anti-gravity tech, legacy of human-AI war. (God: Novatium — Progress & Technology)

Spiritual World: Cultivation of Ki, Prana, Qi; taming spiritual beasts, inner growth. (God: Daonis — Path & Harmony)

Avatar System

Create Avatars in the three other worlds. Avatar achievements strengthen the Main World owner. Semi-divine Avatars can cross worlds directly.

Plot Hook

One universe has already fallen to the Nilids. Gods form a Pact allowing Main World residents to use Avatars for accelerated development. The story begins as first portals open, with a reincarnated gamer from our world as the key hero.

Hero & Mission

The protagonist is a gamer from Earth, reincarnated into the Main World. Tasked by Vaelora, he must unite the four worlds, lead resistance, and stop the Nilids.

Project Goals

  • Build a rich IP across books, animation, games, and media.
  • Engage the community and crowdfunding.
  • Offer an immersive fantasy-sci-fi-cultivation experience.
